Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

NutriRest Alarm ("we", "us", or "our") operates the NutriRest Alarm mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Data Collection and Use

We do NOT collect any personal identifiable information. The application works entirely offline and uses local device storage (Android SharedPreferences) to save your timer settings and session state.

Data Retention

Your settings and session state are stored locally on your device. This data is retained for as long as the application is installed on your device. We do not store any of this data on our servers or in any cloud services.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete all data associated with the application at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the app: Android automatically deletes all local storage (SharedPreferences) associated with the app when it is uninstalled.
  • Clearing App Data: You can manually clear the application's data through your Android device settings (Settings > Apps > NutriRest Alarm > Storage > Clear Data).
  • Stopping a Run: Pressing the "STOP RUN" button within the app immediately clears the active session state (start time and elapsed time).

AdMob

The Service uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain data (such as advertising IDs) to provide personalized ads. For more information, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Foreground Service

The app uses an Android Foreground Service to keep the interval timer running accurately while the screen is off or the app is in the background. This service does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
