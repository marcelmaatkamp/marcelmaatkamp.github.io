Master your freelance finances with this goal-oriented net income calculator for Dutch professionals. This app helps you plan your financial future by calculating the rates you need to achieve your goals.

- Onboarding Wizard
- Hours Tracker
- Tax Buffer Calculation
- Market Comparison
- Full support for Dutch tax rules (ZZP, MKB, KOR)
